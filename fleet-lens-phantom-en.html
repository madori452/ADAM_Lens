ADAM Elements knows aerial photography

We manipulate lights for you

The LensPro Filter Series is comprised of a wide range of neutral density (ND) filters, central polarizing (CPL) filters, ultraviolet (UV) filters, starburst effect (STAR) filters and graduated color (GC) filters of different colors along with several combo sets.The combo packs are perfect for advanced users who'd like to explore more possibilities with less expense, while professionals can freely pick the individual lens combinations that suit them best.In the comprehensive LensPro Series, there are always a few filters for you.

Best design, material and process for best results

Shaper images at all times

The LensPro Series employs premium optical glass, multi-layer coating lens along with aircraft-grade anodized aluminum alloy ring with laser engraving.The extremely high precision and 99.5% transmittance are far superior than similar products with only plastic rings and even lenses in terms of both product and image quality.

A combo set of LensPro filters elevates your works

Light, Strong and Brilliant

Made with lightweight, high-tensile aluminum alloy, the LensPro filters weights 1.3g each; that makes them extremely portable, environment-resistant and have virtually no effect to the drone performance.The lenses all come with a complimentary hardshell case that is very protective for your travel and storage convenience. It's so light you might not even feel it, just like the LensPro to your drone.
